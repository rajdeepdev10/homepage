Inside Neighbourhood Getting Here

8 Dawn street is a 3-story building with six 2-bedroom apartments. Each apartment has an approximate area of 750 sq. ft. Apartments also have a kitchen, a living room, and a washroom. The building does not have an elevetor. It is only possible to access the building only from the front side but it can also be exited using the back side. This building is primarily wooden structure.

The following facilities are available in this building:

  • 24hr laundry ($1.50 per wash)
  • Thermostat
  • Refrigerator (with each apartment)
  • Fire Extinguisher
  • Fire Alarm
