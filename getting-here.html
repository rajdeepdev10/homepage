Inside Neighbourhood Getting Here

8 Dawn Street is located not far from Downtown Halifax and is easily accessible by both public transit and car.

From Downtown Halifax

By Car

Get onto Quinpool Rd (NS Trunk 3) upto Armdale Rotary and then get onto Joseph Howe Drive. From there, take Dutch Village Road which becomes Titus St and take a left on Evans Ave.

By Bus

Take Bus 2 or 4 towards Lacewood Terminal and get off at Evans Ave.

From Stanfield International Airport

By Car

Get onto NS-102 Southbound and take Exit 0 at Joseph Howe Drive. From there, take Dutch Village Road which becomes Titus St and take a left on Evans Ave.

By Bus

Take Bus 320 towards Downtown Halifax and get off at Bridge Terminal. From there, take Bus 2 towards Lacewood Terminal and get off at Evans Avenue
