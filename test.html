Inside Neighbourhood Getting Here

8 Dawn Street is located in the northern end of historic Fairview neighbourhood in Halifax. This area has high concentration of new immigrant communities coming predominantly from South Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. Unfortunately, the human population is surpassed by rat and insects living in the buildings. Some nearby attractions include:

  • Titus Smith Memorial Park (2 min walk)
  • Centennial Arena (4 min walk)
  • Bluenose Mini Mart (1 min walk)
  • Halifax Shopping Center (7 min drive)
  • Chocolate Lake Beach (10 min Drive)
